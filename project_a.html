This technical description outlines the developmental process of machine learning models, intending to inform readers about technology which is becoming more engrained in all of our lives. I've always had a deep interest in AI, and wanted to spread this increasingly crucial skill of understanding how LLM are designed, function, and their drawbacks.. This document uses graphics, metaphors, and simplification to allow this to be more accessible to a larger audience.
